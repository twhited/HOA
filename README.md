## HOA Timeline
### 2023
- 2024 HOA fee is announced as unchanged for 2024 at $35.00 [Source](https://github.com/twhited/HOA/blob/main/Docs/budget/2024%20Budget%20Information%20Stratford%20Station.pdf)

### 2024
- November 1, 2024 - Maintenance fee is increased to $45.00, citing "After carefully considering the economic environment, inflation, rising maintenance costs, escalating costs in general overhead, and any necessary capital improvement projects" [Source](https://github.com/twhited/HOA/blob/main/Docs/budget/2025%20Budget%20Information%20Stratford%20Station.pdf)

### 2025
- November 11, 2025 - Maintenance fee is increased to $75.00, citing "After carefully considering the economic environment, inflation, rising maintenance costs, escalating costs in general overhead, and any necessary capital improvement projects" [Source](https://github.com/twhited/HOA/blob/main/Docs/budget/2026%20Budget%20Information%20Stratford%20Station%20.pdf)
- November 19, 2025 - I reach out to ask about calling a special meeting per the CCRs via a petition, ask when the annual meeting would occur per the CCRs, and also reference the CCRs rules around the maximum allowed increase without calling a vote. [Source](https://github.com/twhited/HOA/blob/main/Messages/11-19%20Personal%20request%20to%20Ghertner.pdf)
