# Stratford Station HOA Financial Analysis (2023-2026)

## 1. Budget Scale and Revenue Trends
* **Total Budget Volatility**: The total budget peaked in 2023 at **$103,740.00**, dropped significantly in 2024 to **$47,820.00** and 2025 to **$47,040.00**, before rebounding to **$91,200.00** in 2026.
* **Maintenance Fee Growth**: Revenue from maintenance fees for single-family homes or general fees has seen a dramatic increase, rising from **$67,620.00** in 2023 to **$88,200.00** in 2026.
* **Working Capital Shifts**: The association relied on Working Capital in 2024 (**$13,800.00**), which was reduced to **$6,000.00** in 2025 and further down to **$3,000.00** in 2026.

## 2. Major Expense Drivers
* **Grounds Care Expansion**: This is the most significant upward trend. Total Grounds Care grew from **$37,900.00** in 2023 to **$51,600.00** in 2026.
* **Specific Contract Growth**: The Grounds Care Contract alone increased from **$15,000.00** in 2023 to **$46,000.00** in 2026.
* **Pond Maintenance Strategy**: Combined pond and detention pond maintenance costs were reduced from **$17,500.00** in 2023 to **$5,500.00** in 2026.
* **Management Fees**: Management costs have remained stable with slight annual increases: **$11,592.00** (2023), **$11,939.76** (2024), **$12,297.95** (2025), and **$12,666.89** (2026).

## 3. Reserve and Long-Term Health
* **Fluctuating Reserve Transfers**: Contributions to reserves dropped from a high of **$37,941.00** in 2023 to **$3,478.24** in 2024, before beginning a recovery to **$10,588.11** in 2026.
* **Fiscal Consistency**: The HOA consistently targets a **$0.00 Net Income**, ensuring all revenue is allocated to operations or reserves each year.
